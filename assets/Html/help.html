A quiz game is an interactive game that tests your knowledge on various topics. It typically involves a series of questions that you need to answer correctly to earn points or progress further in the game. Quiz games can be played individually or in teams, and they often cover a wide range of subjects such as history, science, sports, movies, and more. They can be played in person, online, or through mobile applications. Quiz games are a fun way to challenge yourself, learn new things, and compete with others.
